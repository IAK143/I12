About I12

Pioneering accessible technology for inclusive digital experiences

Our Mission

At I12, we believe technology should adapt to people, not the other way around. Our AR Mouse solution breaks down barriers for individuals with motor impairments, providing them with the tools to interact with digital content naturally and effortlessly.

Founded in 2022 by a team of computer vision experts and accessibility advocates, I12 combines cutting-edge research with deep empathy for user needs.

Vision

A world where physical limitations don't restrict digital access and expression.

Approach

User-centered design with continuous feedback from the disability community.
